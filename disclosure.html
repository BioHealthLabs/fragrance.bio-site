Affiliate Disclosure & Editorial Policy

Fragrance.bio is an independent guide. This page explains how we make money and how we protect editorial integrity.

1) Affiliate disclosure

Some links on this website are affiliate links. If you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission. This helps fund content creation and site maintenance.

Affiliate links are marked with attributes such as rel="nofollow sponsored noopener" and open in a new tab.

2) No paid rankings

We do not accept payment for placement in our tables, lists, or rankings. We do not sell “#1” spots.

3) How ratings work

Our star ratings are editorial and relative. They are designed to highlight trade-offs quickly (goal-fit, usability, and typical performance), not to claim objective truth for every individual.

4) Advertising

If we add display ads in the future, we will update this disclosure. Ads will not influence editorial ratings.
