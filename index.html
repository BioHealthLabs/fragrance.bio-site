Fragrance FAQ

Is “Parfum” always better than EDP or EDT?

No. Concentration can influence strength, but composition matters more. Some EDTs outperform some Parfums.

How many sprays is “safe” for the office?

Start with 1–2. You can add later; you cannot remove an overspray.
