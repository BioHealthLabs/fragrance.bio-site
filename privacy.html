Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Fragrance.bio collects, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

1) Analytics (optional)

We use Google Analytics (GA4) only if you accept analytics in the consent prompt. If you reject, analytics is not loaded and the site still works normally.

Analytics helps us understand which pages are useful, which topics are popular, and where content needs improvement. We configure GA4 to use IP anonymization where supported.

You can change your choice at any time via Privacy settings.

2) Affiliate links

Some outbound links are affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you purchase after clicking. Affiliate links do not require analytics consent.

3) Contact

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and message content. We use it only to respond and to manage support and editorial feedback.

4) Operator

Fragrance.bio is operated by ronarn AS, Norway.
